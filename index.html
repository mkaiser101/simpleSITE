  • Why to use SEECworks

1. Skills must be something that can be demonstrated and seen
2. Focus on the positive! (ex. “Joe can staple.” versus “Joe can’t use a staple remover”)
3. Focus on employment interests, NOT hobbies
4. If interests have not been directly stated, what do their skills tell you? Let skills lead you to interests
5. If jobseeker does not have employment history, think about volunteer service, chores at home, community skills (ex. Budgeting money, scheduling transportation, making their schedule, emailing)

In order to be hired and have a great working expereince, one must discover their intersts, skills, and conditions. To ease this process SEECworks has beeen developed with an easy to use survey that gives admins the results eliminating tiring middle steps.
